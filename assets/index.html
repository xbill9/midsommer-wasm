GLOSSARY & LORE

Midsommar: The Swedish celebration of the Summer Solstice. Dancing around the Maypole (midsommarstång) is mandatory. Don't be late, or the pagan spirits will turn you into a Wicker Man sacrifice!

Systembolaget: The state-owned liquor shop. Swedes queue here on Midsummer Eve. Warning: crowded with very thirsty, aggressive shoppers!

Surströmming: Fermented Baltic herring. It smells so bad it can clear a room and disintegrate enemies in a cloud of biological hazard.

Dalahäst (Dalarna Horse): Traditional wooden horse. In this forest, they are giant, red, and angry.

Bobby Brown: A famous Frank Zappa song that Swedish pub patrons inexplicably sing word-for-word in karaoke.

HOW TO PLAY

1

Move Sven

Use W, A, S, D or arrow keys to run around.

2

Hockey Stick

Press E or Left-Click to swing Sven's trusty hockey stick. Hits enemies close by.

3

Surströmming Bomb

Right-Click or press Space to lob smelly fish cans. Explodes into an AOE damage cloud.

4

Find the Gate

In each level, find the Swedish Gate (or Maypole in lvl 6) to proceed. Eat 🧆 and 🥖 for power-ups!
